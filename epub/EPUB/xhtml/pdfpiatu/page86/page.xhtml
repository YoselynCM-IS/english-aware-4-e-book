Quick Guide to Write a Paragraph

 

To write any kind of text, you have to divide it into paragraphs. Paragraphs are small units formed by a

 

topic sentence, supporting sentences and a concluding sentence. The topic sentence carries the main

 

idea; the supporting sentences explain a bit more in detail the main idea, or they are examples, and

 

the concluding sentence rounds the whole idea.

 

Recycling is a good method to avoid pollution. Recycling is using what you already

 

have to avoid consuming more. For example, if your clothes don’t fit you anymore,

 

donate them to a charity fund. Or bring your own coffee mug if you buy coffee in a

 

cafe. In this way, there’s no need for the cafe to give you a single-use wax-coated

 

cup. If you recycle, you help preserve the balance on Earth!

 

There are different kinds of paragraphs. The main are: descriptive, expository, persuasive and narrative.
